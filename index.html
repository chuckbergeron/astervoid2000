What is Astervoid 2000 ?

Astervoid 2000 is a high energy, local multiplayer action space brawler. It's made to be played with friends (or enemies) on a couch in front of a big TV w/ a decent stereo and a few dual-joystick gamepads (Xbox or PlayStation controllers will do).

Can I broadcast Astervoid 2000?

Sure can. We'd love it if you shared our progress on the game with your viewers. Keep in mind that Astervoid 2000 is still in alpha and will change quite a bit before it's final release. There is a build # (version) in the bottom-right hand corner of the game as you're playing which will be removed for the final version.

I have feedback / questions

Amazing! You can email us, can post them on our Itch community boards, or on the Astervoid subreddit.


Astervoid 2000 will be released November 25th, 2016 on Steam & Itch.io.
